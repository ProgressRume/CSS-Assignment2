Difference between paddings, borders and margins

Padding, Border and Margin are all properties of the CSS box model. Padding refers to the inner space between the content and the border of your box. Border on the other hand refers to the perimeter of the box. This can be invisible or styled to have a thick colored line. Margin is the outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
